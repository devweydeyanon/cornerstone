Common Questions
Frequently asked questions
How quickly will I receive a quote?

We aim to respond within 1 business day to acknowledge your request, and provide a detailed quote within 5 business days. Complex projects may require a site visit before we can finalise pricing.

Do you offer fixed-price contracts?

Yes. After the design phase is complete and specifications are finalised, we provide a fixed-price contract with a detailed line-item budget. Any changes are handled through a transparent change order process.

What areas do you serve?

We operate from our Amsterdam headquarters and take on projects throughout the Netherlands. For exceptional projects, we consider locations in Belgium and Germany.

What is your minimum project size?

We typically take on projects with a construction value of €250,000 or above. For smaller renovation projects, we can recommend trusted partners from our network.

What warranties do you provide?

Every project comes with a 5-year structural warranty and a 2-year defects liability period. We also provide Bouwgarant certification which offers additional consumer protection for residential builds.
