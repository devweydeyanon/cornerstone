About Us
A legacy of building trust since 2003

Cornerstone Build Co. was founded with a clear mission: to raise the standard of construction in the Netherlands. What started as a small residential builder has grown into a full-service construction firm delivering projects across residential, commercial, and heritage sectors.

Our team of 120+ professionals operates from offices in Amsterdam, Rotterdam, and Utrecht. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern building technologies — and we back every project with transparent pricing and a 5-year structural warranty.
